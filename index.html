Every question, answered.

Will this replace me on the phone with my clients?
No. Threshold only answers calls you actually miss — calls that would have gone to voicemail. If you pick up, we never even know it happened.
Do I have to change my phone number?
Never. Your number stays the same. You set your carrier's "no-answer forwarding" to a number we give you. Any call you don't pick up gets handled by your assistant.
How fast does it actually answer?
Three seconds on average, from the moment your phone stops ringing. Fast enough that the caller doesn't even realize they were missed.
Can it really show listings?
Yes. While still on the call, it texts the caller photos, price, square footage, and address of homes that match what they're looking for — in real time.
What if the caller wants to talk to a human?
The assistant introduces itself honestly. If the caller insists, it offers to transfer to your cell, or books a specific callback time. Most callers don't ask — they just want help fast.
How long does setup take?
About five minutes. You forward your missed calls, connect your calendar, and send us your active listings. We handle everything else. You're live the same day.
What if I already have a team or assistant?
Threshold works alongside any team. If you have a human assistant, they handle what they can — Threshold handles the rest (after hours, lunch breaks, when they're on another call).
Do leads know they're talking to a machine?
The assistant introduces itself as your assistant — not as a robot. It speaks naturally and asks follow-up questions. Most callers never question it.
How much does it cost?
$200 a month. Flat. No per-call charges, no setup fees, no tiers. 14-day free trial — no credit card required.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contract, no commitment. Cancel with one click. Turn off the call forwarding and Threshold goes quiet.
